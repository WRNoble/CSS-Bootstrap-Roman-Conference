About Classical Roman Conference

The historic city of Washington D.C. will host the first annual conference

Explore the ancient past that laid the foundations of Western Civilization in this day long conference.

Expert Speakers

Our scholars are some of the most influential people currently in the field of Roman studies.

They wish to share their knowledge of the ancient past so that you and I have greater understanding of our present.

Topics to be discussed

Roman Foundations: The Birth of a Republic The Republic Matures: The Roman Republic in the middle period The Punic Wars: The Birth Pains of Empire Gaius and Marius: The Storm before the Storm Caesar: Rome's New Master Virgil: Propaganda in the age of Augustus
