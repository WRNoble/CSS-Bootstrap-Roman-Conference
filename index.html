About Classical Rome Conference

The beautiful city of Portland, Oregon will be the host city for the first ever Classical Rome Conference!

This year the conference will explore the Roman Republic. The conference will conclude with a special lecture on Virgil and role of propaganda in the first century.

Expert Speakers

Our expert speaker lineup was just announced, so don't wait too long before grabbing your tickets!

Learn from world renown experts and scholars. They are experts in the fields of classical history, poetry, and philology

Also hear from a special speaker on the life and time of one of Rome's greatest poets, Virgil.

What You'll Learn

Foundations: How Rome Became A Republic The Republic Matures: The Roman Republic In The Middle Era The Punic Wars: The Birth Pains Of Empire Gaius and Marius: The Storm Before Caesar: Rome's New Master Virgil & Augustus: Propaganda In The First Century
